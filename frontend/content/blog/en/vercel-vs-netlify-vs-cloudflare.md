## Frontend deployment platforms

Vercel, Netlify, and Cloudflare Pages are the three leading platforms for deploying frontend and JAMstack applications.

## Vercel

Creators of Next.js. Best framework support. Edge Functions, ISR, and built-in analytics. Ideal for Next.js teams.

## Netlify

JAMstack pioneer. Edge Functions, Forms, Identity, and Split Testing. Excellent for content-driven sites with Headless CMS.

## Cloudflare Pages

Integrates with Cloudflare ecosystem. Best global performance with 330-city network. Very competitive pricing.

## Performance comparison

Cloudflare leads in global speed. Vercel offers best Next.js performance with ISR. Netlify excels in build times.

## Pricing

Vercel and Netlify offer generous free tiers. Cloudflare Pages has the best value for high traffic.

## Edge Functions

All three platforms offer edge functions. Vercel uses V8 isolates. Netlify uses Deno. Cloudflare uses Workers.

The choice depends on your tech stack. At Vynta we work with all three platforms and recommend the best fit for each project.
