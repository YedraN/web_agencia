B2B lead generation is one of the biggest marketing challenges. AI is changing the rules, enabling identifying, attracting, and qualifying prospects at a scale and precision impossible manually.

## Prospect identification

AI can analyze millions of public data sources (LinkedIn, corporate websites, databases, industry publications) to identify companies that match your ideal customer profile.

AI models learn what characteristics your best customers have and automatically search for similar prospects.

## Personalization at scale

With AI, you can generate personalized emails, messages, and content for each prospect based on their industry, role, web behavior, and social media activity. Personalization is no longer manual or limited.

## Lead capture chatbots

Advanced AI chatbots can have natural conversations with website visitors, qualify them in real-time, and automatically schedule meetings with the sales team.

## Recommended tools

**Apollo.io**: Contact database with AI for segmentation and enrichment. **Sales Navigator + AI**: LinkedIn enhanced with AI for prospecting. **Clay**: AI-powered lead enrichment and generation platform. **Drift/Intercom**: AI chatbots for capture and qualification.

## Integrated workflow

1. Define your ICP (Ideal Customer Profile) with AI help
2. AI identifies prospects matching the profile
3. Auto-enrich the data
4. Chatbots capture and qualify leads on your website
5. AI personalizes first contacts
6. Qualified leads pass automatically to sales

## Key metrics

Measure cost per qualified lead, lead-to-opportunity conversion rate, pipeline velocity, and lead generation ROI.
