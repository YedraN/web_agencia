## What are Twitter Cards?

Twitter Cards (now X Cards) are meta tags that let you control how your content displays when shared on X.

## Twitter Card types

### Summary Card

Shows title, description, and thumbnail. Ideal for blog articles and general pages.

### Summary Card with Large Image

Similar to Summary but with a larger featured image. Recommended for visual content and products.

### App Card

For promoting mobile applications. Includes direct links to app stores.

### Player Card

For multimedia content: video, audio, and streams. Plays directly in the timeline.

## Required meta tags

twitter:card defines the card type. twitter:site shows the @ handle. twitter:title and twitter:description are X-specific.

## Best practices

Use images at least 1200x675 pixels. Title should not exceed 70 characters. Maximum recommended description is 200 characters.

## Validation

Use X's Card Validator to test your cards before sharing. Verify the image loads correctly.
