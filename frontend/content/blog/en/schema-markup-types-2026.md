## Why use structured data?

Structured data helps Google understand your page content. With Schema Markup you can get rich snippets, carousels, and knowledge panels.

## Most important Schema types in 2026

### Article and NewsArticle

Ideal for blogs and news sites. Includes headline, datePublished, author, and image.

### Product

For e-commerce. Add price, availability, and reviews. Can generate star ratings in search results.

### FAQPage

Generates expandable FAQs in SERP. Highly effective for improving CTR.

### HowTo

Perfect for tutorials. Shows steps, tools needed, and estimated time.

### LocalBusiness

Essential for local businesses. Includes address, phone, hours, and reviews.

### Event

For conferences, webinars, and events. Google can display them in the event panel.

## How to implement

Use JSON-LD instead of microdata. Generate it with Google's Structured Data Markup Helper or Schema Markup Generator.
