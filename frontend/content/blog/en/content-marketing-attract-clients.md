Content marketing is the strategic creation and distribution of valuable, relevant content to attract and retain a clearly defined audience. Unlike interruptive advertising, content marketing earns attention by providing genuine value.

## The Content Marketing Funnel

**Top of funnel (TOFU)** content addresses broad questions and attracts new visitors. Blog posts, infographics, and videos targeting informational keywords. **Middle of funnel (MOFU)** content helps prospects evaluate solutions. Case studies, comparison guides, and webinars. **Bottom of funnel (BOFU)** content drives conversion. Product demos, free trials, consultations, and testimonials.

## Understanding Your Audience

Create detailed buyer personas covering demographics, goals, challenges, and content preferences. What questions do they ask at each stage of their journey? Where do they consume content? Effective content marketing starts with empathy for your audience's needs.

## Repurposing for Maximum Reach

One piece of in-depth content can become a blog post, a LinkedIn carousel, a podcast episode, a YouTube video, an email newsletter, and several social posts. Repurposing extends reach without requiring constant content creation from scratch.

## Distribution Is Half the Battle

Great content with no distribution plan reaches no one. SEO ensures discoverability through search. Email marketing nurtures existing relationships. Social sharing amplifies reach. Paid promotion accelerates results for high-value content.

## Measuring Content ROI

Track beyond vanity metrics. Page views and shares matter, but true ROI connects content to pipeline and revenue. Use UTM parameters, lead tracking, and attribution modeling to understand which content pieces drive business results.
