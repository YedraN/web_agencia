Google has launched Gemini 3, its most ambitious artificial intelligence model to date. With unprecedented integration into the Google ecosystem, it promises to change how we interact with information.

## Key new features

Gemini 3 introduces advanced multimodal capabilities that surpass its predecessor. It can process and reason about real-time video, analyze documents up to 1 million tokens, and generate responses with verifiable citations.

Integration with Google Workspace is total. Gemini 3 operates within Gmail, Docs, Sheets, and Meet as a contextual assistant that understands your workflow without requiring explicit commands.

## Gemini 3 vs competition

Unlike GPT-5 and Claude 4, Gemini 3 leverages Google's search infrastructure. This allows it to access up-to-date information in real-time, significantly reducing hallucinations.

In multimodal reasoning tests, Gemini 3 achieves leading scores. Its ability to extract information from long videos and complex documents gives it a unique advantage in research and analysis.

## Pricing and availability

Gemini 3 is available in three versions: Ultra (full capability), Pro (speed-quality balance), and Nano (mobile devices). Prices range from $19.99/month for Google One AI Premium to custom enterprise plans.

## Key use cases

Academic research, video surveillance analysis, enterprise document automation, and contextual sales assistants are some of the cases where Gemini 3 shows its maximum potential.
