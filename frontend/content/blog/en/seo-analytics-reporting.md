## Why measure SEO?

SEO without measurement is guessing. Data helps you identify what works, what doesn't, and where to invest resources.

## Key KPIs

### Organic traffic

Users arriving from search engines. Segment by landing page, country, and device.

### Rankings

Average position for your target keywords. Monitor visibility in Search Console.

### CTR (Click-Through Rate)

Percentage of impressions resulting in clicks. Low CTR indicates title and description issues.

### Organic conversions

Goals completed by organic traffic: forms, purchases, sign-ups.

## Reporting tools

### Google Search Console

Direct Google data. Clicks, impressions, CTR, and position. Segment by query, page, and country.

### Google Analytics 4

Turn sessions into users and behavior. Use events to measure organic conversions.

### Looker Studio

Create dashboards connecting Search Console + GA4. Visualize trends and alerts.

## Reporting frequency

Weekly: traffic and key positions. Monthly: complete analysis with trends. Quarterly: strategy and roadmap.
