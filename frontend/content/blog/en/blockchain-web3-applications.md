## Beyond cryptocurrencies

Blockchain is a distributed ledger technology with applications far beyond cryptocurrencies.

## Supply chain traceability

Blockchain enables product tracking from origin to consumer. Every transaction is immutable and verifiable.

## Digital identity

Self-sovereign identity without intermediaries. Users control their data and decide what to share.

## Asset tokenization

Represent real-world assets (real estate, art, intellectual property) as transferable digital tokens.

## Electronic voting

Transparent and auditable voting systems. Each vote is recorded on the blockchain and can be verified.

## Decentralized Finance (DeFi)

Lending, exchanges, and insurance without intermediaries. Smart contracts automate financial operations.

## NFTs and digital ownership

Non-fungible tokens representing ownership of unique digital assets: art, music, collectibles.

## Web3 tech stack

Ethereum, Solana, Polygon as base layer. Hardhat and Foundry for development. Ethers.js and Web3.js for interaction.

## Conclusion

Blockchain is a transformative technology. At Vynta we explore practical Web3 applications for businesses: traceability, digital identity, and tokenization.
