## What is equity crowdfunding

It's selling shares of your company through an online platform to multiple retail investors. Unlike reward-based crowdfunding, investors receive real equity in your company.

## Benefits for your startup

Market validation (if people invest, they believe in your project), organic marketing (your investors promote your company), a community of ambassadors, and access to capital without depending on a single fund.

## Main platforms

Crowdcube, Seedrs (UK), StartEngine (US), Wefunder (US), Companisto (Germany). Each has different requirements for revenue, company age, and legal structure.

## How to prepare your campaign

You need a compelling video, a detailed pitch deck, realistic financial projections, a minimum investment amount (usually $10-100), and a story that emotionally connects with the community.

## What investors look for

Traction and team. They want to see recurring revenue, month-over-month growth, and a team that has executed before. Transparency is key: show risks and how you'll mitigate them.

## Legal aspects

Regulations vary by country. In the US, Regulation Crowdfunding (Reg CF) limits raises to $5M per year. In the UK, platforms are regulated by the FCA. Always consult a lawyer.
