## Fast SaaS is possible in 2026

Modern tools let you launch a functional SaaS in weeks, not months. No-code for prototyping, modern frameworks for development, AI for acceleration, and cloud for scaling without infrastructure.

## Tech stack for speed

Frontend: Next.js or Remix for web, React Native for mobile. Backend: Supabase (BaaS) or Firebase. AI: OpenAI API, Claude, or open-source models. Auth: Clerk or Auth0. Payments: Stripe.

## MVP in 4 weeks

Week 1: authentication + basic onboarding. Week 2: core functionality (the feature that solves the main problem). Week 3: payments and billing. Week 4: polish and launch.

## AI as an accelerator

Use Cursor, Lovable, or Copilot to generate code 2-3x faster. Use AI for copy, documentation, tests, and design. It doesn't replace the developer but multiplies their productivity.

## Landing page first

Launch the landing page with pre-registration before having the product. Measure real interest. 500 pre-registrations validate the idea more than any survey. If nobody signs up, don't build yet.

## Launch strategy

Product Hunt, Hacker News, niche communities. Closed beta for first 100 users. Intensive feedback loop in the first week. Pricing based on conversations with beta testers.

## Post-launch

First month: fix bugs and improve onboarding. Second month: add requested features. Third month: publish case study with first results. From month 4, invest in growth.
