## Why webinars work

The webinar is the format that generates the most qualified leads per hour invested. It combines authority (expertise), urgency (live), and depth (long format). Conversion to customer can reach 20%.

## Types of webinars

Educational: teach something useful without selling. Panel: multiple experts discuss a topic. Product demo: show your solution. Workshop: attendees do practical exercises. Educational webinars convert best long-term.

## Planning

Topic your audience actively searches for. Title promising a specific result. Ideal duration: 45-60 minutes. Include Q&A. Best day/time: Tuesday-Wednesday 11am-1pm.

## Promotion

Email your list (2-3 emails). LinkedIn and social media. Partnerships with other creators. Paid ads to lookalike audiences. Promotion should run 2-3 weeks before the event.

## During the webinar

Engagement: live polls, chat questions, reactions. Don't read slides. Be natural and authentic. The offer (if any) comes at the end, after demonstrating value. Never sell at the beginning.

## Post-webinar conversion

Thank you email with recording. Time-limited offer for attendees. Segment by attendance: live attendees are hotter leads. Follow-up sequence of 3-5 emails.

## Repurposing

Reuse the webinar as content: YouTube video, blog article, podcast episode, clips for social media. Each webinar should generate content assets for months.
