Automatic meeting transcription has become an essential tool for teams wanting to capture, search, and share knowledge generated in their conversations.

## How does it work?

AI speech recognition systems convert meeting audio to text in real-time. Current models like OpenAI Whisper or Deepgram solutions offer over 95% accuracy in multiple languages.

## Current capabilities

Modern systems not only transcribe but also identify who is speaking (diarization), generate automatic summaries, extract action items, and detect key topics.

## Main tools

**Otter.ai**: Reference for English meetings. Offers real-time transcription, summaries, and shared notes.

**Fireflies.ai**: Integrates with Zoom, Google Meet, Teams, and Slack. Transcribes, analyzes sentiment, and generates actions.

**Fathom**: Free for basic use. Stands out for accuracy and highlight clip generation.

**Whisper (OpenAI)**: Open-source and free. Ideal if you need to process large volumes or integrate it into your systems.

## Workflow integration

1. Meeting is automatically transcribed
2. AI generates summary and actions
3. Notes saved to your CRM/project manager
4. Team members can search past conversations
5. Knowledge becomes accessible to the entire organization

## Benefits

Teams using automatic transcription report 30% less time in meetings (because they know they are recorded), better follow-up on agreements, and significant reduction in follow-up emails.

## Privacy and security

Verify the tool complies with GDPR and your security policies. Many offer end-to-end encryption and the option not to store recordings.
