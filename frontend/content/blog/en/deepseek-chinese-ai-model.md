DeepSeek has burst onto the artificial intelligence scene as a serious competitor to the American giants. Developed in China, this model has proven that you can compete at the highest level with a fraction of the budget.

## What is DeepSeek?

DeepSeek is a family of language models developed by a Chinese company. Its most powerful model, DeepSeek-V3, has 671B total parameters and uses an MoE architecture that activates only 37B per token.

The most surprising fact is that it was trained with an estimated budget of only $6 million, compared to the hundreds of millions spent by OpenAI or Google.

## Performance and capabilities

On benchmarks like MMLU, HumanEval, and GSM8K, DeepSeek-V3 achieves results comparable to GPT-5 and Claude 4. It particularly excels at programming and mathematical reasoning.

DeepSeek supports a 128K token context window and is fully functional in multiple languages, including Spanish and English with native fluency.

## Access and availability

DeepSeek is available as an API with significantly lower prices than the competition. They also offer open-source versions of their smaller models that you can download and run locally.

## Market implications

DeepSeek's success shows that the technology gap is closing. It is no longer necessary to invest billions to create competitive models. This has profound implications for the AI industry and geopolitics.
