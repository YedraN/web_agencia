Stability AI has released Stable Diffusion 4, its most advanced image generation model. With significant improvements in speed, quality, and control, it positions itself as the reference tool for professional creators.

## Key new features

SD4 introduces real-time generation: you can watch the image build second by second and stop it when you achieve the desired result. Native resolution reaches 2048x2048 without needing upscaling.

The model offers unprecedented control over composition, lighting, and style through Conditioning Bridge, a new architecture that allows guiding generation with visual references.

## Text and image mode

SD4 integrates its own language model that understands complex prompts with multiple subjects and spatial relationships. No more complicated magic word chains: "an orange cat sitting to the right of a blue vase on a wooden table" works perfectly.

## Requirements and availability

Like its predecessors, SD4 is open-source. You can download and run it locally, though requirements have increased: 12GB VRAM for the full version and 6GB for the turbo version.

It is also available on cloud services like Replicate, Hugging Face, and Stability AI Platform.

## Professional use cases

Product design, video game asset generation, A/B test variant creation for marketing, and social media visual content production are some of the areas where SD4 excels.
