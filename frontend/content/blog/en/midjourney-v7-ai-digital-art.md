Midjourney remains the favorite tool of designers and digital artists. Version 7 incorporates substantial improvements that solidify it as a reference in AI-powered visual creation.

## What's new in Midjourney v7

Version 7 introduces region-based editing: you can select specific parts of an image and regenerate them with a new prompt without affecting the rest. This eliminates the need for external editing tools.

It also includes advanced Style Reference, which captures the style of any reference image and applies it consistently to new generations.

## Improved coherence and realism

Midjourney v7 has significantly improved facial and anatomical coherence. Characters maintain their features across multiple variations, and hands and fingers are no longer a problem.

The model also better understands complex spatial relationships and lighting and composition instructions.

## Professional usage

For professional use, Midjourney v7 offers high-resolution modes (up to 4096x4096), batch processing for generating hundreds of variants, and APIs for integration into automated workflows.

The platform still works through Discord, but they have also launched their own website with a more intuitive visual interface.

## Pricing

Plans range from $10/month (200 generations) to $120/month (unlimited generations in relaxed mode) for professional teams.

## Tips for better results

Be specific about lighting, framing, and style. Use visual references whenever you need consistency. Experiment with the stylization parameter to control how much creativity the model adds.
