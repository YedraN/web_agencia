Robotic Process Automation (RPA) combined with artificial intelligence is transforming business efficiency. Together, RPA and AI create intelligent automation that goes beyond repetitive tasks.

## What is RPA?

RPA (Robotic Process Automation) are software bots that mimic human actions in digital systems: filling forms, extracting data from applications, moving files, and executing transactions.

Traditionally, RPA could only work with structured data and fixed rules. AI adds the ability to handle unstructured data and make decisions.

## The difference AI makes

By adding AI to RPA, bots can read unstructured documents (invoices, contracts, emails), understand context, make decisions based on ambiguous content, and learn from human corrections.

A traditional RPA bot needs perfectly formatted data. An AI-powered bot can extract information from a scanned PDF, interpret a natural language email, or classify documents by their content.

## Use cases

**Invoice processing**: RPA extracts data, AI classifies and validates, RPA enters into ERP.

**Customer service**: RPA queries systems, AI analyzes sentiment and generates responses, RPA updates CRM.

**Employee onboarding**: RPA creates accounts, AI generates personalized documentation, RPA sends notifications.

## Step-by-step implementation

1. Identify repetitive, time-consuming processes
2. Evaluate if you need RPA only or RPA+AI
3. Select the platform (UiPath, Automation Anywhere, Power Automate)
4. Design the flow with RPA and AI components
5. Test with real data and adjust
6. Monitor and optimize continuously

## Expected results

Companies implementing RPA+AI report 40-70% reductions in processing time, near-zero errors, and operational costs reduced by 25-50%.
