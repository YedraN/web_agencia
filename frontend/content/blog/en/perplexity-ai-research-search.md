Perplexity AI is redefining what it means to search for information on the internet. Unlike traditional search engines that return links, Perplexity answers questions with complete responses, citing its sources.

## How does Perplexity work?

Perplexity combines a language model with real-time web search. When you ask a question, the system searches the internet, processes results, and generates a synthesized answer with citations to original sources.

Each response includes links to the consulted pages, making it easy to verify information.

## Perplexity Pro vs free

The free version offers unlimited searches with a basic model. Perplexity Pro ($20/month) includes advanced models (GPT-5, Claude 4), file uploads for analysis, and academic database search.

## Professional uses

**Market research**: Get competitive analysis with up-to-date sources in seconds. Perplexity finds and synthesizes information from multiple sources automatically.

**Academic analysis**: The academic search feature allows exploring papers, citations, and peer-reviewed studies.

**Product development**: Research trends, competing technologies, and market requirements without spending hours browsing.

## Collections and organization

Perplexity allows organizing conversations into thematic collections. You can create separate research spaces per project and share them with your team.
