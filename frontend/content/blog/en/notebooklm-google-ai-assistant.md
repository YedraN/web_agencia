Google NotebookLM is a unique tool in the AI ecosystem. Unlike other assistants, it works exclusively with documents you provide, guaranteeing responses based on your sources.

## What makes NotebookLM special?

NotebookLM only uses information from documents you upload. It does not search the internet or use general knowledge. This makes it ideal for academic research, legal analysis, or any task requiring work with specific sources.

You can upload PDFs, Google Docs, web pages, YouTube videos, and text files, and NotebookLM processes them to answer questions about their content.

## Key features

**Audio Overview**: NotebookLM can generate a synthetic podcast with two voices discussing your documents. It is ideal for reviewing material while driving or exercising.

**Document Q&A**: Ask specific questions and receive answers citing relevant passages from your sources.

**Study notes**: Automatically generate summaries, glossaries, and study guides from your documents.

## Professional usage

The most powerful use cases include legal contract analysis, technical documentation review, executive report preparation, and market research synthesis.

The Audio Overview feature is particularly useful for teams that need to catch up on extensive documentation without reading everything.

## Limitations

NotebookLM cannot access the internet, which limits its usefulness for topics requiring up-to-date information. However, for working with closed documentation, it is unmatched.
