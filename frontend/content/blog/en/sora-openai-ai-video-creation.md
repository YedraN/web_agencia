OpenAI has opened Sora to the general public, changing the game in audiovisual content creation. This AI video generation model promises to democratize film production.

## How does Sora work?

Sora is a diffusion model that generates video directly from text descriptions. Unlike previous approaches that generated frame by frame, Sora creates complete scenes understanding physics, lighting, and spatial relationships between objects.

The result is videos up to 60 seconds with cinematic quality, coherent motion, and temporal consistency.

## Current capabilities

Sora can generate videos at resolutions up to 1080p, with styles ranging from realistic to 3D animation. It can also extend existing videos, fill in missing frames, and change visual styles.

One of its most impressive capabilities is generating videos that maintain coherence across multiple shots and angles.

## Limitations to consider

Sora still has limitations: it can produce artifacts in fast movements, struggles with complex physical interactions (like hands grabbing objects), and requires very descriptive prompts for precise results.

## Pricing and access

Sora is available for ChatGPT Plus subscribers ($20/month) with a limit of 50 videos per month. Pro subscribers get 500 monthly videos and 1080p resolution without watermarks.

## Professional applications

Marketing, audiovisual prototyping, social media content, and scene previsualization for traditional production are the most promising use cases.
