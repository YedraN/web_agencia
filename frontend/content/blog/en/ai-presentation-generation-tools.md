Creating engaging presentations takes hours of work. AI is changing this, enabling generation of complete presentations from a prompt or document in minutes.

## Main tools

**Gamma.app**: Leader in AI presentation generation. Creating a presentation from scratch takes less than 5 minutes. Offers visual themes, automatic charts, and PDF/PPT export.

**Beautiful.ai**: Ideal for design teams. Automatically applies design principles to your content. You don't worry about alignment, spacing, or typography.

**Tome.app**: Similar to Gamma, with good storytelling capabilities. Allows importing documents and converting them into presentations.

**ChatGPT + Claude**: You can generate the content and structure, then use Gamma or PowerPoint for visual formatting.

## How to create effective presentations

1. Define the objective and audience before starting
2. Provide detailed content to the AI
3. Review and adjust the generated structure
4. Customize visuals with your brand colors
5. Add your expertise and real cases

## Prompts for best results

Be specific: "Generate a 10-slide presentation about AI in logistics for an audience of operations executives." The more context you give, the better the result.

## Limitations

AI-generated presentations can be generic. For important content, always add your perspective and company-specific data. Review visual coherence and data accuracy.
