## The importance of meta tags

Meta tags are the first impression users get of your site on Google. A good title and description can double your CTR.

## Title tag

The title should be 50-60 characters. Include the primary keyword at the start, the brand at the end, and use separators like | or -.

## Meta description

Between 120-160 characters. It should be an attractive summary including the keyword and an implicit call to action.

## Robots meta tag

Control how search engines index and follow links on your page. Use noindex for internal pages like thank-you or login.

## Viewport meta tag

Essential for responsive design. Ensures your site displays correctly on mobile devices.

## Open Graph and Twitter Cards

These meta tags control how your content displays when shared on social media. Include specific image, title, and description.

## Canonical tag

Indicates the preferred URL when there's duplicate or similar content. Prevents duplicate content penalties.
